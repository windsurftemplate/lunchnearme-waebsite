Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How is this different from Yelp or Google Maps?
Yelp and Google Maps are directories; they give you a list. We are a decision engine. We give you a single, high-confidence recommendation based on your taste, location, and past history.
How does the AI learn my preferences?
When you onboard, we ask a few simple questions. Then, after each recommendation, you can give a thumbs up or down. The more you use it, the better it gets at predicting what you're in the mood for.
What areas do you cover?
LunchNear.me works anywhere in the United States. Our AI uses real-time data to find restaurants near your current location, so whether you're in a major city or a smaller town, we've got you covered.
How does group lunch voting work?
Create a lunch group, invite your coworkers via a shared link, and when it's time to decide, the AI suggests options that work for everyone's preferences and dietary restrictions. Everyone votes, and the winner is chosen in seconds. No more endless Slack debates.
When is the iOS version coming?
We're actively working on the iOS version and it's coming soon! Sign up with your email to be notified the moment it launches. Android users can download and start using the app right now.
Can I use the app for dinner or breakfast?
Absolutely! While we're called LunchNear.me, the app works for any meal. The AI considers time of day when making recommendations, so it'll suggest breakfast spots in the morning and dinner options in the evening.
What if I don't like the recommendation?
Just swipe to get a new one! You can also give a thumbs down to help the AI learn. The more feedback you provide, the better it gets at understanding your taste. Think of it as training your personal food concierge.
Do you support dietary restrictions?
Yes! During onboarding, you can set dietary preferences like vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, halal, kosher, and more. The AI will only recommend restaurants that accommodate your needs.
