Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How is this different from Yelp or Google Maps?

Unlike Yelp or Google Maps where you browse endless options, LunchNear.me uses AI to actually pick for you based on your preferences, mood, and past favorites. We eliminate the paradox of choice that makes lunch decisions so exhausting. Plus, our group voting feature solves team lunch coordination instantly.

How does the AI learn my preferences?

Every time you save a favorite, rate a recommendation, or visit a spot, the AI learns more about your taste. It considers factors like cuisine preferences, price range, distance tolerance, dietary restrictions, and even time of day. The more you use it, the better it gets at predicting what you'll love.

Is the app free?

Yes! LunchNear.me is free to download and use. All core features including AI recommendations, map browsing, favorites, and group lunch voting are completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the essentials will always be free.

What areas do you cover?

We're currently focused on San Francisco, with detailed coverage across downtown, SoMa, FiDi, Mission, Marina, and surrounding neighborhoods. We're actively expanding to more cities. Want us in your city? Let us know through the feedback form!

How does group lunch voting work?

Create a lunch group, invite your coworkers, and when it's time to decide, the AI suggests options that work for everyone's preferences and dietary restrictions. Everyone votes, and the winner is chosen in seconds. No more 47-message Slack threads about where to eat.

When is the iOS version coming?

We're actively working on the iOS version and it's coming soon! Sign up with your email to be notified the moment it launches. Android users can download and use the app right now.
