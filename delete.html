Delete My Data

Request complete deletion of your account and all associated data

What gets deleted?

When you request data deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (email, display name)
  • Profile photo and food preferences
  • All reviews and ratings you've submitted
  • Group memberships and voting history
  • Usage data and location history

This action is permanent

Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use LunchNear.me again.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, please send us an email with your registered email address. We will process your request within 30 days.
