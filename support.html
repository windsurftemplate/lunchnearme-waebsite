Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find restaurants near me?

LunchNear.me uses your device's location to automatically show restaurants nearby. Make sure you've granted location permissions when prompted. You can also search for restaurants in a specific area by using the search feature.

How do I create or join a lunch group?

To create a group, tap the "Groups" tab and select "Create Group." Give your group a name and invite friends via a share link. To join a group, simply tap on an invite link shared with you or enter the group code in the app.

How does the AI recommendation work?

Our AI learns your preferences over time based on your ratings, saved restaurants, and cuisine preferences. The more you use the app, the better the recommendations become. You can also take our food preference quiz to help us understand your tastes faster.

How do I reset my password?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.

Why isn't my location working?

First, check that location services are enabled for LunchNear.me in your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services. On Android, go to Settings > Apps > LunchNear.me > Permissions. Make sure location is set to "While Using the App" or "Always."

How do I delete my account?

You can request account deletion through our data deletion page or by emailing privacy@lunchnear.me. We'll process your request within 30 days.

Is the app free to use?

Yes! LunchNear.me is completely free to download and use. All core features including restaurant discovery, AI recommendations, and group coordination are available at no cost.

The app is running slowly. What can I do?

Try closing and reopening the app, or check for app updates in the App Store or Google Play. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.
